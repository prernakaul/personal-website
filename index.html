Hello!
I am a Senior Technical Product Manager at Amazon working on Alexa Large Language Models. I have a background in engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology and MBA from Rotman School - University of Toronto.
Prior to Amazon, I worked at Walmart Labs where I launched the Scan and Go mobile app across 400 Canadian stores, and at Points.com where I launched their flagship Points Loyalty Wallet.
I'm passionate about machine learning and enjoy building AI/ML products in the healthcare, eCommerce, and enterprise domains.
Outside my day job, I enjoy speaking at tech events, writing, and coaching upcoming tech professionals. I have given 10+ talks on machine learning, platform product management, and careers in tech. I am also a startup mentor at First Round Fast Track. Learn More.
