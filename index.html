Hello!
I am a Senior Technical Product Manager at Amazon working on Alexa Large Language Models. I have a background in engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology and MBA from Rotman School - University of Toronto. I'm passionate about machine learning and have built and launched tech products in healthcare, consumer, and B2B enterprise domains.
